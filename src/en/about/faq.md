## About Sessions and Schedules

### How do online classes work?

We use secure and encrypted video conferencing platforms (Zoom, Google Meet) where students meet their teachers for live, interactive sessions. All you need:
- A device (computer, tablet, or smartphone)
- Stable internet connection
- Headphones and microphone

The teacher shares their screen for presentations, writing, and direct interaction with the student.

### How long is each session?

- **Basic Plan**: 30 minutes
- **Standard Plan**: 45 minutes
- **Premium Plan**: 60 minutes

### How many sessions per week?

Depends on the chosen plan:
- **Basic**: 2 sessions per week
- **Standard**: 3 sessions per week
- **Premium**: 5 sessions per week

### Can I choose session times?

Yes! We offer complete flexibility in choosing times that fit your schedule, whether morning, afternoon, or evening. You can coordinate with your teacher to set the ideal times.

### What if I miss a session or need to reschedule?

You can reschedule any session with **24-hour** advance notice, or get a video recording of the session if you cannot attend.

## About Programs and Curriculum

### What programs are available?

We offer three main programs:

1. **Quran Studies** - Recitation, Tajweed, memorization, and contemplation
2. **Arabic Language** - Reading, writing, grammar, and conversation
3. **Islamic Studies** - Creed, jurisprudence, and Prophetic biography

You can choose one program or combine them.

### What ages do you teach?

We offer programs specially designed for all ages:
- **Children** (5-12 years)
- **Teens** (13-17 years)
- **Adults** (18+ years)

### Do I need prior knowledge of Arabic or Quran?

**Not at all!** We accept complete beginners. We start by assessing your current level then design a learning plan starting from where you are.

### How is the student's level assessed?

- **Free trial session** - Initial level assessment
- **Placement test** - To determine the appropriate starting point
- **Personal interview** - To understand goals and needs
