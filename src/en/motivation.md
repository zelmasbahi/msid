# Our Motivation for Establishing MSID Online

## Our Mission

The **MSID Online** project was launched from our deep belief in the importance of authentic and balanced education that combines Islamic values with contemporary skills.

In a world where life accelerates and challenges increase, knowledge remains an unwavering light and a bridge connecting our children to their roots and identity wherever they are.

## The Challenges We Address

We noticed that children of the Arab and Muslim diaspora abroad face numerous difficulties:

- **Difficulty learning Arabic** in a non-Arabic environment
- **Weak connection to cultural and religious identity**
- **Lack of specialized educational programs** for teaching Quran and Arabic remotely
- **Unavailability of qualified native teachers** in their regions
- **Challenging schedules** that don't fit their daily lives
